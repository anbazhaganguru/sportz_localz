About the Game

Archery is the art and sport of shooting arrows at a target using a bow. It dates back thousands of years as both a survival skill and a form of competition. Modern archery focuses on accuracy, precision, and consistency over varying distances.

Basic Rules

  • Competitors shoot a set number of arrows at a target.
  • Targets are marked with concentric rings, each worth different points.
  • Highest cumulative score across rounds determines the winner.
  • Safety rules are strictly enforced on the range.

Skills Needed

  • Focus and concentration
  • Upper body strength and stability
  • Consistent shooting technique
Quick Info

Players: Individual or teams

Equipment: Bow, arrows, target board

Field Size: Outdoor or indoor shooting range
