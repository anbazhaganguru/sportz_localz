Handball

Fast, furious, and played with actual hands.

About the Game

Handball is a high-speed team sport where two sides pass and dribble a ball with their hands to score goals in the opponent’s net. Played indoors on a court, it combines elements of basketball, soccer, and hockey, with an emphasis on teamwork and speed.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has 7 players: 6 outfield players and 1 goalkeeper.
  • Players can pass, dribble, and shoot using only their hands.
  • Goals are scored by throwing the ball into the opponent’s net.
  • Games are played in two halves, usually 30 minutes each.

Skills Needed

  • Agility and quick reflexes
  • Accurate passing and shooting
  • Team coordination and tactics
Quick Info

Players: 7 per team

Equipment: Handball, indoor court, goals

Field Size: 40m × 20m indoor court
