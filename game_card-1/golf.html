Golf

Clubs, patience, and way too much walking.

About the Game

Golf is a precision sport where players use clubs to hit a ball into a series of holes on a course in as few strokes as possible. It’s played on sprawling courses with varied terrain, testing both accuracy and patience.

Basic Rules

  • Each hole has a par score, representing the expected number of strokes.
  • Players take turns hitting their ball from where it lies.
  • Lowest total strokes across all holes wins the match.
  • Special formats include match play, stroke play, and scrambles.

Skills Needed

  • Accuracy and swing technique
  • Focus and patience
  • Strategic shot planning
Quick Info

Players: Individual or groups

Equipment: Clubs, golf ball, tees

Field Size: 9 or 18-hole course
