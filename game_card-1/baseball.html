About the Game

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of nine players each. The batting team tries to score runs by hitting the ball and running around four bases, while the fielding team aims to get batters and runners out.

Basic Rules

  • The game is divided into nine innings.
  • Each team alternates between batting and fielding each inning.
  • Runs are scored by hitting the ball and safely reaching all four bases.
  • Outs are made by catching the ball, striking out batters, or tagging runners.

Skills Needed

  • Batting power and timing
  • Pitching accuracy
  • Fielding coordination
Quick Info

Players: 9 per side

Equipment: Bat, ball, gloves, bases

Field Size: Diamond-shaped, ~90ft between bases
