About the Game

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It involves batting, bowling, and fielding. The game is famous for lasting anywhere from a few hours to five days, depending on the format.

Basic Rules

  • Each team bats and bowls in turns.
  • Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets.
  • Fielders try to get batters out by catching, bowling, or hitting stumps.

Skills Needed

  • Batting technique
  • Bowling accuracy
  • Fielding speed
Quick Info

Players: 11 per side

Equipment: Bat, ball, stumps

Field Size: Large oval ground
