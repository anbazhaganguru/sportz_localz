About the Game

Cycling is both a competitive sport and a recreational activity, covering disciplines like road racing, track cycling, mountain biking, and BMX. Races test endurance, speed, and tactical awareness, often over long distances or challenging terrains.

Basic Rules

  • Races can be individual or team-based depending on the discipline.
  • Drafting (riding close behind another cyclist) is common in road races.
  • Safety gear like helmets is mandatory in all competitive formats.
  • Winners are decided by fastest time or highest points in stage events.

Skills Needed

  • Stamina and cardiovascular endurance
  • Bike handling and balance
  • Strategic pacing and teamwork
Quick Info

Players: Individual or team

Equipment: Bicycle, helmet, cycling gear

Field Size: Roads, velodromes, or trails
