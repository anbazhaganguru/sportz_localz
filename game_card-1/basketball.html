About the Game

Basketball is a team sport where two teams compete to score points by throwing a ball through the opponent's hoop. It combines speed, strategy, and skill, and is played professionally, recreationally, and in schools worldwide.

Basic Rules

  • Each team tries to score by shooting the ball into the opposing team's basket.
  • Games are played in four quarters, with points awarded for field goals and free throws.
  • Players cannot walk or run while holding the ball without dribbling.
  • Fouls are penalized, and free throws may be awarded.

Skills Needed

  • Agility and speed
  • Teamwork and communication
  • Dribbling, shooting, and passing skills
Quick Info

Players: 5 per team

Equipment: Basketball, hoop, court

Field Size: Standard indoor or outdoor court
