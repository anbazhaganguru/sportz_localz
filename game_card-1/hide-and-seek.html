Hide and Seek

Count to ten, pretend you don’t peek, and then chase your friends.

About the Game

Hide and Seek is a classic children’s game played worldwide. One player closes their eyes and counts while the others hide. The seeker then tries to find all the hidden players.

Basic Rules

  • One player is chosen as the seeker and counts with eyes closed.
  • Other players hide in the designated area.
  • The seeker must find all hidden players; the first caught usually becomes the next seeker.

Skills Needed

  • Stealth and patience
  • Quick thinking for hiding spots
  • Observation for seekers
Quick Info

Players: 3 or more

Equipment: None

Field Size: Flexible (yard, park, house)
