Tag / Catch

Run fast, don’t get caught — childhood survival training.

About the Game

Tag, also called Catch, is one of the simplest outdoor games. One player is “it” and must chase and tag the others. The tagged player becomes “it,” and the game continues until everyone collapses laughing (or exhausted).

Basic Rules

  • One player starts as “it.”
  • “It” must chase and tag other players by touching them.
  • The tagged player becomes the new “it.”

Skills Needed

  • Speed and agility
  • Dodging and reflexes
  • Endurance (and sometimes luck)
Quick Info

Players: 3 or more

Equipment: None

Field Size: Any open space
