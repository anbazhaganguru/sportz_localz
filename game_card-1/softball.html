Softball

A bigger ball, a shorter field, but no less competitive.

About the Game

Softball is a bat-and-ball game similar to baseball but played on a smaller field with a larger ball. It is often played recreationally, but competitive leagues exist worldwide. The game focuses on teamwork, strategy, and quick reflexes.

Basic Rules

  • Each team fields 9 or 10 players depending on the format.
  • The game is usually 7 innings long.
  • Pitches are delivered underhand rather than overhand.
  • Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running around four bases.

Skills Needed

  • Strong batting technique
  • Quick fielding and throwing
  • Base running speed
Quick Info

Players: 9–10 per side

Equipment: Bat, ball, gloves, bases

Field Size: Diamond-shaped, ~60ft between bases
