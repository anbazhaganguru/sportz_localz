Badminton

Fast rallies, light rackets, and the mighty shuttlecock.

About the Game

Badminton is a racket sport played with a shuttlecock, which must be hit across a net into the opponent’s court. It can be played as singles (1v1) or doubles (2v2). The sport demands agility, precision, and lightning-fast reflexes.

Basic Rules

  • Matches are played best of 3 games, each to 21 points.
  • Players score points when the shuttlecock lands in the opponent’s court or when the opponent commits a fault.
  • The shuttle must always be hit below the waist on a serve.
  • Only one hit is allowed per side before returning the shuttle.

Skills Needed

  • Quick footwork
  • Strong wrist and arm control
  • Strategic shot placement
Quick Info
