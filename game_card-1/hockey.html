Hockey

Fast sticks, flying balls, and plenty of bruises.

About the Game

Hockey is a team sport where players use curved sticks to control a ball (or puck, depending on the version) and score by getting it into the opposing goal. It is known for speed, skill, and a lot of running.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has 11 players in field hockey.
  • Players may only hit the ball with the flat side of the stick.
  • Goals are scored by getting the ball into the opposing goal from within the shooting circle.

Skills Needed

  • Stick handling and dribbling
  • Passing and coordination
  • Endurance and agility
Quick Info

Players: 11 per side

Equipment: Stick, ball, protective gear

Field Size: Rectangular turf, ~100m x 60m
